

### Privacy Policy for Group Left Tabs

#### Introduction
We respect the privacy of any users of Group Left Tabs Chrome extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our extension. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the extension.

#### Information Collection
Our extension does not collect any personal or sensitive user data. It operates fully within the confines of your device and does not transmit any data externally. 
**The extension requires the following permissions**: ["tabs", "tabGroups"] to function effectively. These permissions are used solely for the purpose of enhancing your browsing experience by organizing your tabs in Google Chrome.

#### Use of Information
As we do not collect personal or sensitive user data, there is no use of such data.

#### Information Sharing
Since no personal or sensitive user data is collected, no such data is shared with any third parties.

#### Data Security
We take your security seriously and take reasonable measures to protect any data from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Our extension transmits data over a secure connection and uses modern cryptography standards to ensure data protection.

#### User Data Handling Compliance
Our extension complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, particularly the Limited Use requirements. We do not use or transfer any user data for purposes other than the core functionality of the extension as described in the Chrome Web Store listing.

#### User Consent
By using our extension, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

#### Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
